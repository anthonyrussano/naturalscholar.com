# Mulberry

<img src="https://res.cloudinary.com/alchemist-cookbook/image/upload/w_200,f_auto/healing-items/mulberry.jpg" style="border-radius: 5px; float:left; margin: 5px;">**Mulberry**[^1]

## Healing Properties

Mulberry fruit supports weight loss and protects against fatty liver disease

Mulberry fruit has been reported to effectively reduce body weight by preventing visceral fat accumulation and limiting food intake.  Dried Mulberry fruit Powder supplementation has anti-obesity properties and also improves liver lipid profile, thus it can be developed as a food supplement for weight control and prevention against liver lipid accumulation (fatty liver disease) resulting from a high fat diet

Source: [Naresuan University, Thailand](http://www.journal.nu.ac.th/NUJST/article/view/Vol-29-No-2-2021-43-53)
