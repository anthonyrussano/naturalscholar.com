### [Alpha Lipoic Acid](./research/lipoic-acid)

#### Attenuates Inflammatory Response in the Brain

[Konyang University, Daejeon, Korea | Korean Journal of Clinical Laboratory Science](https://doi.org/10.15324/kjcls.2020.52.3.253)
    
(Sept 30, 2020) Î±-Lipoic Acid is a therapeutic agent against neuroinflammatory responses.  Alpha-Lipoic Acid has anti-dementia and anti-Alzheimer's Disease properties due to several actions, such as scavenging the reactive oxygen species (ROS), reducing inflammatory processes, and regenerating endogenous antioxidants.
